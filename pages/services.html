Our Well-Known Services

Hospitality Groundworks offers a comprehensive range of hospitality services within the hospitality , tourism and leisure industries including start-up advisory service, feasibiltiy studies, concept planning, property managment, asset and finance management, recruitment and trianing etc.

Advisory Services

Feasibility Studies: A feasibility study is an excellent investment for new and existing businesses as it can assess the viability of a development project from the start and determine operational gaps with recommendations on how to optimize operations and gain competitive edge for existing businesses. Our feasibility studies will provide you a clear insight into development risks, profitability opportunities and ROI, market gaps, critical project timelines which will you avoid pitfalls and achieve sustainability.

Concept Design Development: Each project requires a tailored design concept which is unique to the brand and in line with the business needs. At this stage, we will work hand-in-hand with the client and design team to develop the concept, review procurement options, cost plans, buildability and construction logistics, programme and phasing strategy etc.

Aesthetic Development: Aesthetics are an integral part of our industry. Customer satisfaction now goes beyond delivering good service as developing an atmosphere and ambience that compliments your brand identity, operations and space is critical to creating a wholesome customer experience which will lead to brand loyalty and repeat patronage. With our multidisciplinary expertise we know what works and what doesn’t and can guide you through the development of a fantastic atmosphere and a profitable venue.

Procurement and Vendor Sourcing: We have extensive sourcing and procurement experience from connecting producers, distributors and clients. Our wide network of highly skilled interdisciplinary professionals within supporting sectors such as manufacturing, food & beverage, interior design etc. allows us to source for any materials required for each project both locally and internationally.

Operational Service Review: At Hospitality Groundworks, we understand the importance of well-structured operations and are well equipped in assisting business to optimise operations. We help to evaluate performance, allocate resources, set time frames and introduce cost effective measures that ultimately lead to better streamlined processes, efficiency, shortened timelines, increased productivity, high quality outputs and on-time & on-budget deliveries.

Revenue Management: We will identify your business challenges and recommend effective and sustainable solutions to bridge gaps in management. We will also forecast demands and proffer supply chain solutions as well as establish solid pricing management.

Facility Management

On-site Management: We offer fulltime and part time on-site management of client’s properties. With our hands-on experience we keep our client’s properties operating at peak performance levels, ensuring business functionality, maintaining extensive property lifecycle and customer satisfaction.

Pre-opening Consulting: We relieve our clients of the burden of the often complicated pre-opening procedures. With our hands-on approach, we oversee the entire preopening process, from branding, inventory purchasing to site inspections, scheduling progress meetings, developing budgets etc.

Post-opening Consulting: We carry out thorough review of our clients finished products to identify any deficiencies and develop resolutions plans to tackle them.

Human Resource Consulting

We take full responsibility of merging our clients’ company mission and vison with professional standards to develop a healthy and well defined corporate culture. We oversee the recruitment and training process ensuring that our client has credible and high skilled employees that will contribute to the success of the business.
